About Me

I am an aspiring physician-scientist passionate about the intersection of medicine, research, and health equity.
My interests lie in understanding disease at the molecular level, particularly infectious diseases and how environmental factors shape disease progression and treatment outcomes.
Through my academic, research, and leadership experiences, I have developed a strong commitment to advancing equitable healthcare and contributing to research that addresses both global and underserved populations.
I aim to pursue an MD-PhD to integrate clinical care with scientific discovery and create meaningful impact in medicine.
